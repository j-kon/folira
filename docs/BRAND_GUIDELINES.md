# Folira Brand Guidelines

> **Read anything. Even offline.**  
> *Your private library, always within reach.*

---

## 1. Brand Concept & Story

Folira is a private, personal reading library for people who want to keep documents, books, reports, notes, and learning materials organized on their own device.

It is designed as **a quiet personal reading space that travels with you**.

### Core Attributes
* **Calm**: Uncluttered, distraction-free environment that puts reading front and center.
* **Intelligent**: Smart document metadata, automatic page progress tracking, and fingerprinting.
* **Private**: 100% device-local storage via IndexedDB. Zero cloud tracking, analytics, or external servers.
* **Warm**: Thoughtfully curated warm paper and slate palettes (`#F8F5EE`, `#2F6B4F`, `#C89545`).
* **Bookish**: Editorial typography pairing sans-serif interface elements with rich serif reading accents.

---

## 2. Logo System

The Folira visual mark combines four symbolic concepts:
1. **An open or folded page**: Represents reading and papercraft.
2. **A subtle letter "F"**: Brand identifier.
3. **A bookmark tab**: Represents progress and saved location.
4. **A doorway or reading space**: Represents privacy and sanctuary.

### Clear Space & Minimum Sizes
* **Minimum favicon size**: 16 × 16 px
* **Browser icon size**: 32 × 32 px
* **Interface icon size**: 48 × 48 px
* **PWA app icons**: 192 × 192 px & 512 × 512 px
* **Clear Space**: Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the width of the inner bookmark tab (`0.5x`) around the logo mark on all sides.

## 3. Brand Color System

### Primary Colors
* **Folira Forest (`#2F6B4F`)**: Primary brand color, used for brand marks, primary buttons, and key focus states.
* **Deep Forest (`#204B38`)**: Hover and active states for primary actions.
* **Soft Leaf (`#DDEBE2`)**: Subtle background fills and selection highlights.

### Neutral Colors
* **Paper (`#F8F5EE`)**: Primary warm background tone for light mode and paper theme.
* **Warm White (`#FFFDF8`)**: Card backgrounds and clean elevated surfaces.
* **Soft Stone (`#E8E5DD`)**: Subtle borders, dividers, and container edges.
* **Graphite (`#252A27`)**: Primary body text color, offering high contrast without harsh pure black.
* **Deep Ink (`#151A17`)**: Night theme background and dark mode base.

### Supporting Colors
* **Reading Gold (`#C89545`)**: Bookmark highlights, favorite stars, and warm accent badges.
* **Soft Gold (`#F2E4CB`)**: Sepia reading theme background.

---

## 4. Typography System

Folira uses a dual typeface system:
1. **Inter** (Sans-Serif): Modern, highly readable UI font used for interface navigation, buttons, metadata, toolbars, and forms.
2. **Literata** (Serif): Rich editorial font used for welcome headlines, featured quotes, and reading accent elements.

Both fonts are bundled locally as WOFF2 webfonts to guarantee 100% offline functionality.
